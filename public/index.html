Benefits We Offer

Continuous Monitoring

Through monitoring using office IP cameras, WellnessBuddy's artificial intelligence analyzes each employee's behavior patterns, allowing them to stay alert at all times when a worker shows signs of mental health issues, such as stress among others.

Real-Time Alerts

When the platform's AI detects any unusual behavior pattern, it sends a series of alerts to the corresponding app section, so that the person in charge of checking the WellnessBuddy account is immediately informed of the situation.

Personalized Recommendations

The platform's AI offers a series of recommendations based on the collected data of the worker who is facing mental health problems, this to provide some possible solutions that are useful for the problem.

Access to Mental Health Resources

The application has a section where users will be able to access mental health resources that can be sent to employees automatically via their corporate email, so that they are properly informed about useful possible methods of dealing with various mental health problems.
